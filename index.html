  • Most Costly per Unit: Routing errors ($15 per review) due to complex investigation and rework
  • Most Preventable: Incomplete data entry (18%) - fixable with better UX and upfront validation
  • Late Detection Impact: Reconciliation issues (12%) discovered hours/days after submission
  • Total Manual Touch Rate: 73% of payments need intervention at some stage (industry benchmark: 25-35%)
